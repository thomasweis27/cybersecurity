# Determining Assessment Scope and Categories

## Determining Assessment Scope
The assessor must determine:

- The **CMMC assessment scope**
- The **asset categories** included within scope

> Always reference the **correct and most current version** of the applicable CMMC and assessment documentation.

---

## Key Supporting Roles and Service Providers

### Cybersecurity Consultants
- Provide subject matter expertise.
- Assist with control implementation and interpretation.

---

### Managed Service Providers (MSPs)
Typically provide:

- System maintenance
- Operational support
- Security-focused services

---

### Enterprise Network Administrators
Responsible for the management and maintenance of enterprise infrastructure, such as:

- Firewalls  
- IDS/IPS  
- Managed switches  
- DHCP  
- Active Directory  
- Network segmentation and access control  

---

### Cloud Security
Defined by Gartner as:

> Processes, mechanisms, and services used to control security, compliance, and usage risks associated with cloud computing.

---

### Security as a Service (SECaaS)
- Third-party providers delivering security capabilities.
- Security services are integrated into the contractor's infrastructure.
- Typically subscription-based.
